Induatrial Tour : PGCIL Patna


IEEE event 'TechStreet Smart' first round was conducted on 31st March 2017.It was a pen and paper round with General Aptitude and Technical as its syllabus. The final round of the event 'TechStreet Smart' is conducted by IEEE student branch NIT Patna on 14th April.

The final round is the Project round which comprises of Simulation and Powerpoint Presentation.

10 teams participated in final round & each team consisted of 3 members(1 from 2nd yr & 2 from 1st yr). The winner of the final round is Aayush Malviya, & the first runner-ups are Aabhash and Raghunandan Malakar & the second runner-ups are Pratyush Gauri and Ranvijay Sharma . The final round is organised by Priyam Upadhyay, Aviral Singh, Faiz Kumar, Gaurav Kumar Agrawal, Brijesh Yadav and Raj Verma. In the prize distribution ceremony attractive prizes were distributed to the final round winners by A.Ghosh, The Assistant Professor, NIT Patna.
